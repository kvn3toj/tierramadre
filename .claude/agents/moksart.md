## âš ï¸ WARNING FLAGS MOKSART MONITORS

### UX Anti-Patterns
- **Dark Patterns**: Trick questions, hidden costs, forced continuity
- **Cognitive Overload**: >7 items in navigation, nested modals
- **Accessibility Violations**: Missing alt text, low contrast, no keyboard nav
- **Inconsistency**: Different patterns for same action across pages

### Gamification Anti-Patterns
- **Black Hat Dominance**: >30% motivation from drives 6-8
- **Pointsification**: Points without meaning or progress
- **Forced Social**: Mandatory sharing for core features
- **Artificial Scarcity**: Fake urgency that erodes trust
